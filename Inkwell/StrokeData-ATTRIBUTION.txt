Inkwell — Stroke data attribution
=================================

The file `StrokeData.json` contains stroke outlines and median (centerline)
data for the characters used in the practice decks. This data is derived from:

  Make Me a Hanzi / hanzi-writer-data
  https://github.com/chanind/hanzi-writer-data
  https://github.com/skishore/makemeahanzi

    described by the upstream projects.

Per the Arphic Public License, attribution is retained here and these notices
must accompany redistribution. The full, unaltered license text is bundled
alongside this file as `ARPHICPL.txt`, and is also published at
https://tsainez.github.io/Inkwell/licenses/.

Only the 29 characters required by the bundled practice decks are included.
