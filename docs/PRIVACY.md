# Inkwell Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 4, 2026

Inkwell is an iPad app for practicing Chinese and Japanese character
handwriting. It is designed so that your data never leaves your device.

## What Inkwell stores

Inkwell stores the following information **only on your device**:

- **Settings and preferences** — grading strictness, guide-grid style, hint
  sensitivity, and appearance (light/dark), stored in the app's local
  preferences.
- **Practice progress** — per-character statistics such as how many times you
  have practiced a character and how many stroke corrections you made, stored
  in a local on-device database so you can review your progress over time.

## What Inkwell does *not* do

- **No data collection.** Inkwell does not collect, transmit, or share any
  personal information or usage data.
- **No accounts.** There is nothing to sign up for and no login.
- **No network access.** All character stroke reference data is bundled with
  the app. Inkwell does not connect to any server.
- **No analytics, no ads, no tracking.** Inkwell contains no third-party
  SDKs, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

## Your data, your control

Because everything lives on your device, you are in full control:

- You can erase all practice history at any time from
  **Settings → Data → Reset all progress** inside the app.
- Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.
- If your device backs up to iCloud or your computer, Inkwell's local data is
  included in that backup under the terms of Apple's backup services; Inkwell
  itself never uploads anything.

## Children's privacy

Inkwell does not collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of Inkwell ever changes how data is handled (for
example, optional iCloud sync of your progress), this policy will be updated
first and the change will be described in the App Store release notes.
