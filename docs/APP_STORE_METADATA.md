## Description (4,000 char limit)

Inkstone turns your iPad into a practice sheet for Chinese hanzi and Japanese kanji. Write each character with Apple Pencil and get instant feedback on every stroke — order, direction, and shape — so bad habits never take root.

FEEDBACK ON EVERY STROKE
Most apps just play an animation and hope you follow along. Inkstone watches you write. The moment you finish a stroke, it's checked against the real reference form:

• Out of order? You'll know immediately.
• Drew it backwards — right to left instead of left to right? Flagged.
• Shape too far off? Try it again.

Choose strict grading when you want discipline, or lenient mode while you're finding your feet.

PRACTICE ANY CHARACTER
Start with the built-in decks — first kanji from JLPT N5, HSK 1 essentials, and the numbers shared by both scripts — or type in any character to practice exactly what you're studying. Stroke data for thousands of characters ships inside the app, so everything works fully offline. No connection, no waiting.

TRACK YOUR MASTERY
Inkstone remembers every character you practice. Browse your full mastery grid to see what you've learned and what needs another pass, check your lifetime stats, and let the Character of the Day nudge you into a quick session.

MADE FOR iPAD AND APPLE PENCIL
Inkstone is built iPad-first around PencilKit, so writing feels immediate and precise — like practicing on grid paper, minus the eraser shavings.

DESIGNED LIKE INK ON PAPER
A warm, calm ink-and-paper aesthetic in both light and dark mode. No clutter, no gamified noise — just you and the character.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account. No ads. No tracking. Your practice history stays on your device.

Whether you're starting your first HSK list, grinding through kanji for the JLPT, or just want your handwriting to stop embarrassing you, Inkstone gives you the one thing textbooks can't: honest feedback on every single stroke.
